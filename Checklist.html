Mental health checklist

  • I spent 5 minutes focusing on my breathing.
  • I wrote down one thing I’m excited about.
  • I practiced positive self-talk for 1 minute.
  • I smiled at myself in the mirror today.
  • I wrote down 3 things I’m grateful for.
  • I called a friend or family member.
  • I went for a 10-minute walk outside.
  • I drank a glass of water this morning.
  • I unplugged from social media for 30 minutes.
  • I read a chapter of a book I enjoy.
  • I spent 10 minutes doing something creative.
  • I stretched or meditated before bed.
  • I spent 5 minutes organizing my space.
  • I listened to my favorite song.
  • I turned off my phone for 1 hour.
